Dental Services

Gum Disease Treatment

Gum disease, also known as periodontal disease, is a condition that affects the gums and bone supporting the teeth. Our comprehensive gum disease treatment aims to halt the progression of the disease and maintain oral health. Services include:

Periodontal Cleaning (Root Surface Debridement)

A thorough cleaning of the teeth and gums to remove plaque and tartar from above and below the gum line. This treatment is crucial for controlling the bacteria that cause gum disease. This can be spread across multiple appointments depending on the clinical need.

Periodontal Maintainence

Regular follow-up visits are scheduled to monitor the health of your gums and ensure the effectiveness of the initial treatment. These visits may include additional cleanings and assessments of your gum health.

Dental Hygiene & Airflow

Our dental hygiene services are designed not only to prevent oral diseases but also to enhance the aesthetic appearance of your smile. By combining routine care with innovative treatments, we provide a holistic approach to dental health. Services include:

Routine Scale and Polish Treatments

Regular cleaning to remove plaque and tartar, followed by polishing to smooth the tooth surface, helping to prevent gum disease and decay.

Airflow Treatment:

An advanced cleaning technique that effectively removes stains and discoloration using a gentle stream of water, air, and fine powder. Ideal for those looking to brighten their smile without harsh chemicals or abrasives.

.

Oral Health Assessments and Oral Hygiene Plans

Comprehensive evaluations to monitor the health of your teeth and gums, including risk assessments for oral diseases and screenings for oral cancer. Personalised advice and strategies designed to help you achieve and maintain optimal oral health at home, including the use of appropriate oral hygiene products.

Dental Restorations & Aesthetics

We understand that every smile is unique, which is why we offer a wide range of restorative and cosmetic dental therapies designed to enhance and protect your oral health. Our services are tailored to address both functional needs and aesthetic desires, ensuring that your smile is not only healthy but also bright and beautiful. Our treatments include:

Posterior Composites

High-quality, tooth-colored fillings that restore functionality and appearance to the back teeth. These materials are chosen for their strength and compatibility with the natural tooth structure.

Anterior Composite Bonding

This versatile and minimally invasive procedure repairs and improves the appearance of front teeth, addressing issues such as chips, fractures, discoloration, and minor alignment problems. Composite bonding can transform your smile quickly and effectively, with results that can significantly enhance your overall appearance.

Fluoride Treatment

Essential for strengthening the tooth enamel and preventing decay, our professional fluoride treatments offer an added layer of protection for your teeth, helping to keep them strong and resistant to cavities.

Teeth Whitening

For those looking to brighten their smile, we offer professional teeth whitening services that safely and effectively lift stains and discoloration from your teeth, resulting in a brighter, more confident smile.
